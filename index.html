ClassEdge learning Idea Documentation

A platform to in-person class sessions to the digital world

Overview

This system is designed for educational institutions, specifically for instructors and lecturers, to create interactive and engaging lessons that closely mimic the experience of in-person classroom sessions. It is a versatile platform that can cater to all programs offered by the institution.

Instructor Experience

The instructor experience is at the core of this system. Instructors have the freedom to craft lessons step by step, with tools available for every aspect of lesson creation. This includes:

  • Customizable lesson flow without a strict order
  • Integration of multimedia content
  • Real-time preview of lessons
  • Interactive tools to enhance student engagement

Student Experience

For students, the system offers a seamless and intuitive learning experience. Students can:

  • Search for their program from a wide range of offerings
  • Select a course within the chosen program
  • Engage with lessons that have been carefully prepared by their instructors
  • Take quizzes generated by AI based on the lesson content

This tailored experience ensures that students can access the content they need and learn at their own pace while staying engaged through interactive elements.

Features

This system includes a variety of powerful features:

  • Multi-program and multi-course support
  • Flexible and customizable lesson creation tools
  • Multimedia content integration (images, videos, etc.)
  • AI-powered quiz generation
  • Cross-institution adaptability for a tailored experience
